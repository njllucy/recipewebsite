Vegetable Soup

Vegetable Soup Recipe is one of the soups people enjoy. This soup is a healthy delicious soup you can enjoy with any swallow of choice. Italian sausage in the skillet in the beginning, before the onion and garlic.The is also a popular Nigerian soup recipe prepared in event organized in Nigeria.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

2 servings

Tags : beef breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

Wash the beef and stock fish,put in a clean pot Add slices of onion, seasoning cube and salt. Combine and place on a low heat and leave to cook for 5 minutes

step 2

Add kpomo or cow skin,add little water and cook for 10 minutes Add smoked fish and crayfish,leave for 5 minutes Remove from heat and set aside

step 3

In a separate clean dry pot,put shredded water leaves Add onion slices, crayfish and palm oil and cook on a low for 10 to 15 minutes or when the water leaf is no longer slimy.

step 4

Add the protein to the water leaf,add the remaining oil,ogiri,salt and seasoning cubes, then combine well Leave for 5 minutes,add the shredded ugu leaves,combine and leave for 3 to 4 minutes Remove from heat. The soup is ready!

ingredients

3 medium stock fish ear or to taste

400g kpomo or cowskin

600g smoked fish

4 medium small onions

3 seasoning cubes

2 small custard cups shredded water leaves

3 and 1/2 small custard cups shredded ugu leaves

tools

pot

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
