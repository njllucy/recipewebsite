Guava Mojito's

prep time

5 mins.

Total time

5 mins.

servings

1 serving

Tags : home about catagory

instructions

step 1

Fill a glass with ice.

step 2

Muddle 3 sprigs mint and sugar together in a cocktail shaker; add ice, rum, lime juice, and guava nectar. Cover and shake 10 times. Strain drink into glass filled with ice; add club soda and garnish with 1 mint sprig.

Halloween Jell-O Shots

These Halloween jello shots look like candy corn and make a fun, boozy treat for adults. This version goes for a triple shot, so drink responsibly. Serve with candy corn on the side. Cheers!.

prep time

5 mins.

Total time

5 mins.

servings

1 serving

Tags : home about catagory

instructions

step 1

Make the yellow layer: Pour boiling water into a small bowl. Add lemon gelatin mix and stir until dissolved.

step 2

Pour vodka into a 2-cup liquid measure; add enough ice to make 1 1/4 cups. Pour into lemon gelatin mixture and stir until slightly thickened; remove any unmelted ice. Pour mixture into 16 tall shot glasses, filling each 1/3 full.Refrigerate until lemon gelatin is set, at least 1 1/2 hours.

step 3

When lemon gelatin is set, make the orange layer: Pour boiling water into a small bowl. Add orange gelatin mix and stir until dissolved.

step 4

When orange gelatin is set, make the white layer: Stir warm water and unflavored gelatin together in a bowl until gelatin is dissolved. Stir in condensed milk and rum. Pour into shot glasses over orange gelatin. Refrigerate until set, at least 30 minutes.
