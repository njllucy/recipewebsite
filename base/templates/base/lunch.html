Spicy Pasta

This pasta dish layers flavors and can be made in under 30 minutes, add a salad and crusty bread and dinner is done.

prep time

5 mins.

Total time

5 mins.

servings

1 serving

Tags : home about catagory

instructions

step 1

Bring a large pot with water and 1 tablespoon salt to a boil. Cook rigatoni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until tender yet firm to the bite, about 10 minutes. Drain and reserve pasta water.

step 2

Meanwhile, combine tapioca starch, Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper in a bowl. Add chicken pieces and toss to coat.

step 3

Heat olive oil in a skillet until it shimmers. Add chicken pieces and cook until browned, about 5 minutes. You might be tempted to add more olive oil or butter, don't do this..

step 4

Pour in reserved pasta water and add mushrooms, artichoke hearts, sun-dried tomatoes, and garlic. Cook about 5 minutes. Mix in white wine, cream and Cheddar cheese. Whisk until sauce is slightly thickened. Add rigatoni and toss to combine. Add pasta and toss to combine. Serve immediately.

ingredients

1 (8 ounce) package rigatoni pasta

1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces

1 teaspoon Italian seasoning

¼ cup heavy whipping cream 2 ounces shredded aged Cheddar cheese

2 tablespoons chopped oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes 3 cloves minced garlic

2 ounces shredded aged Cheddar cheese

Grilled Chicken

This fried chicken tenders recipe makes crispy, delicious chicken pieces, paired with our own spicy horseradish dipping sauce. We serve it with our favorite potato salad and corn on the cob.

prep time

25 mins.

Total time

45 mins.

servings

2 serving

Tags : home about catagory

instructions

step 1

Prepare the breading station: Place flour in a shallow bowl. Place eggs and water in a second shallow bowl. Place bread crumbs in a third shallow bowl, and mix in ground black pepper and cayenne pepper.

step 2

Coat chicken in flour, then eggs, and then bread crumbs, one piece at a time, and set aside.

step 3

Heat oil in a deep fryer to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

step 4

Fry chicken in small batches until the pieces are golden brown, 6 to 8 minutes. Remove chicken to drain on paper towels or a wire rack. Make the dipping sauce: Combine mayonnaise, sour cream, horseradish, mustard, and Worcestershire sauce in a small bowl. Mix well, and serve with chicken.

ingredients

24 chicken tenderloins

½ teaspoon ground black pepper

2 large eggs, beaten

2 cups Italian-style seasoned bread crumbs

2 quarts oil for frying

Fried Rice

A quick fried rice recipe like you get at your favorite Chinese restaurant. Leftover rice, plus a couple of eggs, baby carrots, peas, and soy sauce is all you need. Garnish with sliced green onions, if desired.

prep time

5 mins.

Total time

5 mins.

servings

1 serving

Tags : home about catagory

instructions

step 1

Place carrots in a small saucepan and cover with water. Bring to a low boil and cook for 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in peas, then immediately drain in a colander.

step 2

Heat a wok over high heat. Pour in vegetable oil, then stir in carrots, peas, and garlic; cook for about 30 seconds. Add eggs; stir quickly to scramble eggs with vegetables.

step 3

Stir in cooked rice. Add soy sauce and toss rice to coat. Drizzle with sesame oil and toss again.

step 4

Serve hot and enjoy!

ingredients

⅔ cup chopped baby carrots

½ cup frozen green peas

2 tablespoons vegetable oil

1 clove garlic, minced, or to taste (Optional)

3 cups leftover cooked white rice

1 tablespoon soy sauce, or more to taste

Paneer Curry

When we were kids, whenever my parents went out to an adults only party, my mother used to ask us what we wanted for dinner at home. 99% of the time, our choice was pooris and shahi paneer. My mother was always nice enough to oblige. Here is the recipe. It is quite rich and hence a nice treat.

prep time

45 mins.

Total time

5 mins.

servings

1 serving

Tags : home about catagory

instructions

step 1

Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook the onion and garlic in the hot oil until the onions are soft and golden brown, about 5 minutes. Sprinkle the cumin, coriander, turmeric, and chili powder over the onion and garlic; continue cooking until the seasonings are fragrant, about 30 seconds.

step 2

Pour the pureed tomatoes into the skillet; cook until the excess liquid evaporates and the oil separates, 3 to 5 minutes. Add the paneer, water, sugar, and salt to the mixture; stir gently so the paneer does not break apart. Cook until the paneer begins to absorb some of the liquid, about 10 minutes. Stir the cream into the mixture and simmer another 5 minutes. Garnish with cilantro to serve.

ingredients

2 tablespoons cooking oil

1 large onion, thinly sliced

4 cloves garlic, minced

½ teaspoon ground turmeric

1 teaspoon ground coriander

½ teaspoon Kashmiri red chili powder

4 tomatoes, pureed

1 teaspoon white sugar

2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro

salt to taste
